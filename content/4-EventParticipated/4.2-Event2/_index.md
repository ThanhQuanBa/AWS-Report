# Event Report: “AI-Driven Development Life Cycle: Reimagining Software Engineering”

## Date & Location

- **Date:** Friday, October 3, 2025 (2:00 PM – 4:30 PM)
- **Location:** AWS Event Hall, L26 Bitexco Tower, Ho Chi Minh City

## Instructors & Coordinators

- **Instructors:** Toan Huynh & My Nguyen
- **Coordinators:** Diem My, Dai Truong, Dinh Nguyen

## Event Purpose

- Explore how **Generative AI** transforms software development.
- Understand how AI can be integrated into the **software development lifecycle**: architecture, development, testing, deployment, and maintenance.
- Learn how automation using AI reduces repetitive tasks, increases productivity, and allows developers to focus on higher-value creative work.
- Hands-on demonstrations of **Amazon Q Developer** and **Kiro** tools for AI-driven development.

## Agenda

- **2:00 PM – 2:15 PM:** Welcome
- **2:15 PM – 3:30 PM:** AI-Driven Development Life Cycle overview & Amazon Q Developer demonstration (Toan Huynh)
- **3:30 PM – 3:45 PM:** Break
- **3:45 PM – 4:30 PM:** Kiro demonstration (My Nguyen)

## Key Highlights

### AI-Driven Development Life Cycle

- Overview of integrating **Generative AI** into software engineering.
- Automating repetitive tasks and improving productivity.
- Enhancing creative problem-solving by freeing developers from undifferentiated heavy lifting.

### Hands-on Demonstrations

- **Amazon Q Developer:** Demonstrated AI-assisted software development from architecture to deployment.
- **Kiro:** Showcased AI capabilities for development workflows, improving efficiency and accuracy.

## Key Learnings

- Generative AI can **reimagine traditional software development**, making it more intelligent and efficient.
- Developers can leverage AI for **planning, coding, testing, deployment, and maintenance**, reducing manual effort.
- Hands-on experience with Amazon Q Developer and Kiro demonstrates practical applications of AI in real-world development environments.

## Workplace Applications

- Implement AI-driven automation in software projects to increase productivity.
- Use AI tools like Amazon Q Developer and Kiro to **accelerate development cycles** and improve code quality.
- Enhance software team collaboration by leveraging AI-assisted workflow optimization.

## Event Experience

- **Practical & Interactive:** Participants gained firsthand experience with AI-driven development tools.
- **Insightful Learning:** Instructors shared best practices and real-life use cases for integrating AI in software engineering.
- **Networking:** Opportunity to connect with peers and coordinators to exchange knowledge on AI applications in development.
